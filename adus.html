What We Build

Purpose-Built for Your Property

Every ADU we design starts with your specific lot, your zoning, and your goals - not a standard template adapted to fit. Here's what's possible.

Detached Coach Homes

A fully independent structure in your backyard - its own entrance, mechanical systems, and legal suite designation. Maximum privacy for tenants or family, and typically commands the highest rental rates of any ADU type.

Above-Garage Suites

An ADU built above an existing or new garage - often the most structurally efficient and cost-effective solution for adding a legal unit to your property. Self-contained with its own entrance and completely separate from the main home.

Basement & Secondary Suites

A fully legal, permitted interior suite - separate entrance, fire separation, egress windows, and full mechanical compliance. Built correctly and permitted properly. The most accessible entry point for most homeowners.

Laneway Homes

Compact, fully independent homes built along the rear lane of a property. Available in select Lower Mainland municipalities, increasingly enabled through BC's SSMUH legislation. Fully independent and typically separately metered.

Multi-Generational Suites

Connected suites designed for family use - private enough to feel separate, close enough to matter. We design for the real rhythms of multi-generational living: acoustic separation, private entrances, and shared outdoor space where it makes sense.

SSMUH-Enabled ADUs

Where BC's new zoning rules permit multiple units, we assess your lot, confirm eligibility, and design to the full development envelope. A thorough property assessment is always the first step before any planning dollars are spent.

Who Benefits from an ADU

Rental Income Seekers

You own property in one of the most expensive rental markets in Canada. An ADU converts that underused space into meaningful monthly income - enough to offset your mortgage, build long-term wealth, or simply provide financial flexibility you've been working toward. The numbers make sense here more than almost anywhere else in BC.

Families with Aging Parents

Close - but not too close. An ADU is the most practical way to house aging parents or returning adult children with real privacy for everyone. Independent living with the comfort of proximity. Purpose-built for the way modern families actually want to live across generations.

Long-Term Property Investors

Properties with legal, permitted ADUs command meaningful premiums at resale. Buyers in today's market increasingly prioritize income-generating potential. A quality ADU doesn't just add a suite - it changes what your property fundamentally is, and how buyers and appraisers see it.
