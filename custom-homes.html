Custom Homes

A Home That's Truly Yours - Without the Guesswork.

Building a custom home is one of the most significant decisions you'll ever make. It's also one of the most mismanaged. Budget surprises, design decisions made without real cost awareness, and builders who overpromise and underdeliver have become the industry standard - and they don't have to be.

Koze takes a fundamentally different approach. We start with your budget and your goals, not a floor plan. Every plan, every selection, every detail is developed around what's achievable for you - so that by the time you sign a contract, there are no surprises left.

What We Build

Every Element, Designed for You

A Koze custom home is not a spec house with upgraded options. It's a home conceived around your lot, your lifestyle, and your investment - designed and built start to finish by one team.

01

Site-Specific Floor Plans

Every layout is designed around your actual lot - orientation, topography, views, and setbacks - not adapted from a generic plan. We work with your site, not against it, to maximize livability and long-term value.

02

Custom Exterior Design

From roofline to cladding, fenestration to garage integration - every exterior decision is made cohesively, with an eye toward curb appeal, neighbourhood context, and materials that hold up beautifully over time.

03

Interior Architecture & Finishes

Custom millwork, architectural ceiling details, built-in storage, and finish selections chosen for quality and longevity. We coordinate interior design alongside architecture so every detail is resolved before construction begins.

04

Built-In Cabinetry & Millwork

Kitchens, bathrooms, mudrooms, home offices, and libraries - all built to fit the exact dimensions of your space. No filler panels, no standard-sized compromises. Every unit is designed and built for where it lives.

05

Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing

Systems designed for efficiency, longevity, and comfort - not minimum code. We plan HVAC, electrical, and plumbing alongside the architecture so everything is integrated from the start, not retrofitted after the fact.

06

Outdoor Living & Landscaping

Covered decks, patios, and outdoor kitchen areas designed as extensions of the home - not afterthoughts. We plan for outdoor living during design so structural and utility connections are built in from the beginning.

The Koze Difference

A Better Way to Build

The traditional approach to custom home building was not designed with the client in mind. Ours is.

The Traditional Approach The Koze Way
Budget Setting Design first, price later - often well above expectations. Budget is aligned and agreed before a single line is drawn.
Contract Type Cost-plus or allowance-based estimates that expand during construction. Fixed-price contract issued after all scope and selections are finalized.
Project Team Separate architect, interior designer, and GC - often in conflict. One integrated design-build team, one point of contact throughout.
Design Decisions Made without real-time construction cost awareness. Made with the builder in the room - costs are always front of mind.
Cost Surprises Common - change orders and scope creep are standard practice. Eliminated - your price is locked before a shovel breaks ground.
Communication Multiple vendors to chase, conflicting information, gaps in accountability. Proactive updates from one team - you never have to wonder what's next.
Is This Right For You?

Who Benefits from a Custom Home

Growing Families

You've outgrown your current home and you're not willing to compromise on layout, room count, or the way your family actually lives. You want a home that was built around your daily routine - not one you're adapting your life to fit. A Koze custom home gives you every square foot by intention, and none by accident.

Downsizers & Right-Sizers

You're ready to trade square footage for quality - a smaller home, built exactly the way you want it, with no compromises on the things that matter most. Whether you're simplifying for the next chapter or finally building the home you've been planning for decades, a custom build lets you get every detail right.

Landowners & Investors

You own a lot or are purchasing one, and you want to maximize its value with a purpose-built home. A well-designed, well-constructed custom home consistently commands a premium - and with Koze's fixed-price process, you know your costs before you commit. No guesswork. No surprises. Just a clear plan and a result you can bank on.
