Our Process

Why Process Matters

Most Build Stress Comes From Not Knowing What's Next.

The stories you've heard about custom builds and renovations gone wrong, the budget surprises, the contractor who goes silent, the price that keeps creeping, almost always trace back to the same root cause. There was no clearly defined process from the start.

When there's no structure, clients are left guessing. Design decisions get made without budget context. Scope creeps without explanation. And you end up managing a project you hired a builder to manage for you.

One of the reasons Koze is one of the Lower Mainland's trusted design-build firms is that we do so much more than just build. Our team is with you every step of the way, from initial vision, through design, construction, and all the way to handover. That's why you shouldn't wait until you have full architectural plans to call us. The value we provide multiplies the earlier you bring us in.

Five Steps. One Price. No Surprises.

Every Koze project follows the same structured process, designed around the moments where builds typically go wrong, and built to prevent each one.

Step 01: Initial Consultation

We Start With You.

We begin with an in-depth discussion to understand your vision, your needs, and your budget. This is not a sales pitch, it's a real conversation. We provide honest advice and insights to shape your project before anything is committed to paper.

Most builders wait for you to arrive with full plans. We believe the value we provide multiplies when you bring us in from the very start, before the design is locked in and before costly assumptions are made.

  • Clear understanding of your goals, lifestyle, and budget
  • Honest read on what's realistic for your investment
  • Expert guidance on project scope and direction before design begins
  • No obligation, just a real conversation
Step 02: Design & Engineering

The Right Team Around Your Vision.

We've built strong working relationships with the Lower Mainland's premier designers and engineers. We make the introductions, matching the best fit for your proposed style and needs. Once retained, we collaborate closely with your design team to create a home that fits within all of your desired function, design, and cost expectations.

Because we're involved from day one, design decisions are made with construction costs in mind. This alignment is why our budgets are more accurate and our projects more predictable than the traditional approach of hiring separately.

  • Introductions to vetted architects, interior designers, and engineers
  • Collaborative design process, budget stays front and centre throughout
  • Floor plans, exterior concepts, and interior selections coordinated
  • Full review before moving to permitting
Step 03: Permitting

We Handle the Complexity.

Koze manages the permit application process with a professional and coordinated approach to keep things moving. We oversee the preparation and submission of permit documents, coordinate with required consultants and design professionals, and act as the central point of contact throughout the review process.

Our team works closely with municipalities, responds to plan check comments, coordinates revisions, and tracks progress to help avoid unnecessary delays. By managing this process carefully and proactively, we bring clarity to one of the most complex stages of any build.

  • Full permit document preparation and submission managed
  • Coordination with all required consultants
  • Municipality liaison, we respond to comments and track revisions
  • You stay informed without having to manage the process yourself
Step 04: Construction

We Build What We Promised.

At Koze, we handle all aspects of construction, serving as the single point of contact for all subcontractors, consultants, vendors, and city inspectors. Issues are resolved as they arise, not passed along to you. Construction is overseen by your Project Manager, who is on-site daily ensuring all work is completed to specification, using the highest quality building materials and techniques.

With a fixed price and finalized plans already in place, construction begins with clarity on both sides. You receive regular updates throughout, and never have to wonder what's happening on your site.

  • Single point of contact for all trades, vendors, and inspectors
  • Dedicated Project Manager on-site daily
  • Regular progress updates throughout every phase
  • Issues resolved proactively, never passed on to you
Step 05: Handover

Done Right. Fully Documented.

Our in-depth project close-out process includes a full inspection of your completed home. Jason Craig personally joins you and your Project Manager for a detailed final walkthrough to compile the punch list, every item reviewed and resolved before the keys change hands.

Upon move-in, you receive a Comprehensive Close-Out Package: copies of all contracts, invoices, releases, city documents, manuals, warranties, and photographic documentation of your project. This is what a properly closed project looks like.

  • Full final inspection before walkthrough
  • Detailed punch list walkthrough with Jason and your PM
  • Comprehensive Close-Out Package, all documents, warranties, and photos
  • Support available after move-in, we don't disappear at handover
Why It Works

One Team Changes Everything.

In traditional construction, the architect designs what looks good on paper. The contractor builds at the lowest cost. The client sits in the middle managing conflicts between professionals who have never worked together and have no shared accountability for the outcome.

In Koze's design-build model, design decisions are made with construction costs in mind from the start, because the same team is responsible for both. This is why our budgets are more accurate, our timelines more predictable, and our results more consistent.

  • A fixed-scope budget established before a single permit is pulled
  • One point of contact from first conversation to final walkthrough
  • Design that stays protected through every phase of construction
  • Vetted trade partners held to a consistent standard on every project
  • Proactive communication, you're never left wondering what's happening
  • A finished home that reflects exactly what you envisioned at the start
