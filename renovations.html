Scope of Work

Every Element, Reimagined

A Koze renovation isn't cosmetic. Every project is scoped, designed, and priced as an integrated whole - so nothing is treated as an afterthought.

01

Kitchen Transformations

Full kitchen redesign - cabinetry, appliances, countertops, lighting, and layout. We design around how you actually cook and entertain, not around what fits a standard template. Every kitchen is drawn for your specific space.

02

Bathroom Renovations

Primary ensuites, main baths, and powder rooms - tiled, finished, and specified to match the quality of the home. Custom showers, heated floors, custom vanities, and finishes selected to age gracefully.

03

Main Floor Reimagination

Open-concept conversions, structural wall removals, new flooring, and complete layout changes that transform how your home lives and flows. We handle the structural engineering and permits so you don't have to.

04

Primary Suite & Bedrooms

Expanded bedrooms, custom built-ins, walk-in closet systems, and ensuite additions designed around how you actually use the space - not around what a previous owner decided decades ago.

05

Mechanical, Electrical & Structural

Updated panels, HVAC, plumbing rerouting, and structural work - done correctly the first time, not patched around existing problems. We integrate systems improvements into the renovation scope from the start.

06

Secondary Suites & ADU Additions

Legal basement suites, above-garage suites, and coach home additions built to code and permitted for rental or family use. Designed to maximize income potential and add lasting value to your property.

The Koze Difference

A Better Way to Renovate

The traditional renovation process is fragmented, expensive, and full of surprises. Ours is built differently from the start.

The Typical Renovation The Koze Way
Budget Setting Design first, price later - cost balloons well beyond expectations. Budget confirmed before design begins - no surprises, no rework.
Contract Type Cost-plus estimates and open allowances that expand mid-construction. Fixed-price contract after all scope and selections are fully defined.
Trade Coordination Multiple vendors with no single point of accountability or scheduling. One team manages every trade, every decision, every timeline.
Design Decisions Made without knowing what things cost to build. Made with the builder in the room - costs are always front of mind.
Scope Clarity Vague line items and allowances replaced mid-construction with extras. Fully defined scope - your price covers exactly what's been specified.
Communication Multiple contractors to chase, unclear timelines, gaps in accountability. Proactive updates from one team - you always know what's next.
Is This Right For You?

Who Benefits from a Major Renovation

Long-Term Homeowners

You've been in your home for years. The location is right, the bones are good - but the layout, finishes, and systems haven't kept pace with how you live. A well-executed renovation doesn't just update a house. It unlocks the potential in a home you already love, and extends its life for another generation.

Growing Families

The house you bought for two doesn't work for four. You need more space, better flow, and a home that anticipates the next decade - not just the next year. A Koze renovation designs around your family's actual routines, not a builder's standard plan from twenty years ago.

Pre-Sale Investors

The right renovation adds more value than it costs. We help owners understand which improvements command real premiums at resale, execute them to a quality standard buyers notice, and deliver a finished result that competes at the top of its market - not just a refreshed version of the existing home.

These are projects that require permits, design integration, and trade sequencing. We're not the right fit for cosmetic surface updates or single-room refreshes.
