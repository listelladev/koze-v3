Turn Your Lot Into More Than a Single Home

BC's new zoning rules have made more configurations possible than ever. Here's what we design and build under the SSMUH framework across the Lower Mainland.

Duplexes

Side-by-side or stacked configurations, designed for owner-occupy plus rental or pure investment. Two fully contained, legal units on a single lot: designed around your goals, not a builder's template.

Triplexes

Three-unit developments sized and configured to maximize your lot's potential under current bylaws. Each unit purpose-designed for rentability, long-term maintenance, and durable tenant appeal.

Fourplexes

The maximum density on most standard BC lots under SSMUH. Four fully legal, permitted units, designed around return on investment, not compromised to squeeze into an envelope.

Common Questions

SSMUH FAQ

Small-Scale Multi-Unit Housing (SSMUH) refers to residential development enabled by BC's Bill 44 (Housing Statutes Amendment Act, 2023). It allows property owners to build multiple residential units on lots previously zoned for single-family homes, without a full rezoning application. The legislation applies broadly across BC municipalities with populations over 5,000 and was designed to meaningfully increase housing supply on existing residential lots.

In most cases, no rezoning is required. That's precisely what Bill 44 changed. Most standard lots can now accommodate up to 4 units by right. Properties within 400 metres of a frequent transit stop may permit up to 6. Smaller lots under 280 m² may be limited to 3 units. The exact number depends on your lot size, municipality, and proximity to transit. We assess this as part of our property review.

Most standard single-family lots in Lower Mainland municipalities qualify. The legislation was designed to apply broadly. That said, site-specific factors matter: lot dimensions, existing easements, heritage designations, flood plain status, and servicing capacity can all affect what's buildable. The most reliable answer comes from a property assessment. Tell us your address and goals and we'll give you a straightforward picture of your options.
